A powerful Google Apps Script that transforms your Google Sheets into a contact sync hub 
for 8 major marketing and analytics platforms. Upload contacts with one click, track 
upload status, and manage duplicate prevention - all without leaving Google Sheets.

## What It Does

**Sync contacts from Google Sheets to:**
- **CRM/Marketing Automation**: Braze, MoEngage
- **BSP/WhatsApp Business**: Infobip, Bitbybit, Gupshup, Mekari Qontak
- **CDP/Analytics**: Mixpanel, Segment

**Key Features:**
- One-click uploads from Google Sheets menu
- Phone normalization for Indonesia (+62 E.164 format)
- Duplicate detection (marks "Already in Database")
- Status tracking with upload timestamps
- Batch processing for large datasets
- Connection verification before upload
- Upload statistics with progress bars
- Recent contact lists (Infobip, Mixpanel)
